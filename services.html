Our Services

End-to-end staffing & recruiting solutions

Our service includes a comprehensive consultation to help identify your dream job and opportunities — for candidates and employers alike.

What We Offer

Tailored solutions for every hiring need

Whether you're a candidate looking for your next opportunity or a company scaling your team, we've got you covered.

Candidate Placement

We match qualified IT professionals with the right companies. Our recruiters handle the entire process — from sourcing and vetting to interview prep and offer negotiation. There is no charge for candidates, ever.

Employer Staffing

We handle your entire hiring process from start to finish — finding qualified candidates, conducting initial screenings, and presenting only the best talent. Your team stays focused while we handle the search.

Resume & Portfolio Coaching

Our recruiters provide honest feedback on your resume, advice on how to highlight your talents, and guidance on tailoring your application to specific positions. We help your portfolio stand out.

Contract Staffing (W2 & C2C)

We offer flexible staffing arrangements — W2 employment or Corp-to-Corp contracts — tailored to match both client needs and candidate preferences. Fast placements, typically within 48 hours.

Our Process

How we work with clients

Once you decide to work with us, we take care of everything. Here's what our engagement looks like:

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to thoroughly understand your staffing needs, culture, and timeline.

2

Candidate Sourcing

We tap our extensive network and database to identify the best-fit candidates for your roles.

3

Screening & Vetting

4

Placement & Follow-up

We manage the offer process and stay in touch to ensure a smooth onboarding and satisfaction.
